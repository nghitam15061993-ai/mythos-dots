## 🧩 The mint = a word race
One single gate: a timed word-guessing game. Everyone plays the same rules.

2. **Tap to Play** → a hidden English word shows as blanks. Every **3.5 seconds** one more letter is revealed.

## 🔒 Solve = lock (no snipe)
- When you guess right, the protocol **reserves your quota for your wallet** and you mint it on the spot (pay-at-lock).
- Your allocation is **bound to your wallet** — no one can steal it between solving and minting.
- So the competition lives at the **solving** step. First to solve, first to lock. Once the pool is fully locked, later solvers simply miss out.
